# Northwind Retail — Customer Policy Handbook

## Refunds and Returns

A cancelled order is eligible for a full refund within 30 days of purchase. Refunds are issued to the original payment method and may take 5 to 10 business days to appear. Items marked final sale are not eligible for return. To start a return, contact support with your order number.

## Shipping

Standard domestic shipping takes 3 to 5 business days. Express shipping is available at checkout and delivers within 1 to 2 business days. We do not currently offer international shipping. Orders placed after 2pm are processed the following business day.

## Partner Discounts

Only verified partners qualify for the partner discount of 15 percent. Verification requires a valid business registration number and takes up to 3 working days to approve. Partner discounts cannot be combined with promotional codes.

## Account and Security

Customers can reset their password from the login page using the "Forgot password" link. We never ask for your password by email or phone. Accounts are locked after five failed login attempts and can be unlocked by contacting support.

## Support Hours

Our support team is available Monday to Friday, 9am to 5pm. We are closed on public holidays. Urgent issues outside these hours can be submitted through the online form and are answered the next business day.

## Loyalty Program

Members earn one point for every dollar spent. Points expire 12 months after they are earned. 100 points can be redeemed for a 5 dollar credit. Membership is free and automatic for registered customers.

## Order Tracking

Order codes use the format NW-XXXX. Code NW-4471 refers to standard domestic deliveries. Code NW-4472 refers to express deliveries. Code NW-4473 refers to international partner shipments.
